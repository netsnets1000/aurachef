Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

Your game saves automatically on your device. To back it up and play across devices, open Profile → Cloud Save → Sign in and enter your email — we'll send you a magic sign-in link (no password needed).

I switched phones. How do I get my progress back?

On the new device, tap "Have an account? Sign in" on the title screen (or Profile → Sign in) and use the same email. Your cloud save will load automatically.

How do I turn the music or sound off?

Go to Profile → Sound & Music and toggle Sound Effects or Background Music.

How do I delete my account or reset my progress?

  • Reset progress: Profile → Start Over.
  • Delete your account and all data: Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and saves from our servers and cannot be undone.

Is Aura Chef free?

Yes — Aura Chef is completely free to play, with no ads and no in-app purchases.
